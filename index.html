TECHNOLOGY


- Apple Launches Latest iPhone Models -

Article by @ArizonaGeek


The world famous Cupertino, CA company "Apple" (NASDAQ: APPL)
does it again by wowing the world with yet the latest in iPhone innovations. Company CEO, Tim Cook says that the "iPhone Coding Planet Edition" is just the latest in a number of planned launches for this fall. Customers were quick to comment on the phone's amazing ability to be unlocked by simply "feeling your presence". Although the phone does require a small blood sample to setup the feature, customers say it's a small price to pay for peace of mind. The new Coding Planet edition is expected to launch with a tiny 2TB hard drive along with a dual SLI setup for two GTX 1080 TI's inside the device's small wukandan steel frame.


Although the device is expected to be released in October in just 10 years, customers are already camping outside of Apple Stores. We spoke to one excited early worm outside the Apple store near our offices in New York who said "I'm really excited you know, I'm 21 right now so when I'm 31 I can rest assured I have the latest technology for at least the next 6 months after that". Prices are set reasonably for these new models with an entry model with 12kb of on-board storage expected to retail for just one easy payment of $29.99 and 12 not so easy paments of $9,999.99 being charged once every full moon if it lands on the second Friday of that respective month.





Coding Planet user @ArizonGeek has reached out to Apple for a statement, they failed to respond to our media queries citing they were too busy swimming in the Scrooge McDuck gold vault.
